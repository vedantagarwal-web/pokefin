Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a single model. Orthogonal is a multi-agent system where specialized AI analysts (fundamental, technical, sentiment, risk) debate every stock. We use real-time financial data from 10+ sources, not training data from years ago.

Where does the data come from?

Financial Datasets AI (real-time prices, financials), Exa AI (SEC filings, earnings materials, institutional positions), and social sentiment from Reddit/Twitter. All sources are cited with direct links.

Is this financial advice?

No. Orthogonal is a research tool for educational purposes. We provide analysis and insights, but you should always do your own due diligence and consult a licensed financial advisor before making investment decisions.

How accurate are the recommendations?

Our multi-agent debate system provides nuanced analysis with conviction scores. We show you both bull and bear cases, full debate transcript, and risk assessment. The goal is transparency, not guarantees.

What stocks can I research?

Any US-listed stock with a ticker. We support NYSE, NASDAQ, and major exchanges. Coverage includes financials, tech, healthcare, energy, and more.
